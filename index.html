Apa Itu LDC?

Language Development Center (LDC) Institut Teknologi PLN merupakan laboratorium pengembangan bahasa Inggris yang terdapat di IT-PLN. LDC menyediakan beberapa layanan yang bertujuan untuk meningkatkan kemampuan berbahasa Inggris staff, dosen, dan mahasiswa IT-PLN.

Visi

To become a strategic partner in building global competitiveness through English language competencies.

Misi

  • Providing english services for lecturers, student, and staff, including courses, academic, tests, and translation services.
  • Continuously developing our human capital to ensure delivery of quality and reliable English services.
  • Growing a sustainable level of skill and interest towards the English language on and off campus to increase global awareness and competitiveness.
  • Maintaning good and transparent governance to uphold the integrity of English Digital Lab.
